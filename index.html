    Welcome to Kitten World.
    It is a tiny little world.

  • You command the red kittens . Your job is to surround the
    Zombie Kitten King with three red kittens. This will win you the game.
  • If three zombie kittens surround your king, you will lose.
  • If your kittens eat 5 fishes, another kitten will spawn.
  • Fishes randomly spawn in the world.
  • Zombie kittens eat fishes, but they do not multiply.
  • If a zombie kitten touches one of your kittens,
    your kitten will die.
  • If two of your kittens touches one zombie kitten,
    that zombie kitten will die.

    Suggestions for your Kittens

  • While you cannot control your kittens directly, you
    can give them "suggestions".
  • Order your kittens to to stop them from moving.
  • Order your kittens to to start randomly
    moving around, gathering fishes to build your numbers.
  • Order your kittens to to move in the general direction
    of the Zombie Kitten King, in order to destroy him.

    Selling your kittens

  • You can one of your kittens for the ability to
    destroy any wall you click on.

    Stepping the game

  • You can the game one turn at a time, or you can
    to loop the game automatically.
